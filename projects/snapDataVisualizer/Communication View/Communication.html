Communication View

In this view you are able to see information on your communication history.

The charts include the number of Snaps and Chats sent and recieved in the past month aswell as a pie chart of the media types used. This only includes the data from past month.

Below the charts is a collection of all users that the account has interacted with. If you hover over a username you can see when your account connected with their's (if the data is available).

Lastly, all call logs are included in one of the expandable lists.
(Hover over a call to see details)
