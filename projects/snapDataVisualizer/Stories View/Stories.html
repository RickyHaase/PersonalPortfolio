Information on Stories Data

The first chart (views) describes what stories you have viewed in the past month. Stories indicates how many times you've clicked on the user's stories while Snaps and Videos indicates how many of each media type was included in those stories (some stories are made up of 3 videos or 1 snap, etc.)

The second chart (Analytics) shows how many views and replies your stories recieved on each given day throughout the month.

Below the charts, you can see what discover channels you have viewed or are subscribed to.
