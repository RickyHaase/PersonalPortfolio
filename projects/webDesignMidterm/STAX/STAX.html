The STAX SR-L700 earspeakers are among the most unique headphone types in the world. Creating the original set of electrostatic headphones in the early 1960s, the Japanese company STAX has been the leader in electrostatic production for years. Their technology provides incredibly intricate detail and a massive soundstage that rivals almost any other headphone.

The L700 earspeakers remain in their own league within the headphone world. Bringing renowned audio reviewers and connoisseurs to tears, STAX use their unique electrostatic technology to create the sensation of the listener wearing speakers. This enables nearly perfect audio reproduction and lifelike imaging, and the most sensitive details in music are easily heard.
